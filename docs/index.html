Biography

I am an assistant professor at Zhongguancun Academy, affiliated with the AI Theory and Algorithm Division in the AI Core Department. Previously, I was a researcher at Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence (BAAI), Health Computing Group. I received my Ph.D. degree from the College of Artificial Intelligence of Xi'an Jiaotong University, advised by Prof. Nanning Zheng. I also received my B.Eng. degree from the Faculty of Electronic and Information Engineering of Xi’an Jiaotong University. During my doctoral studies, I was part of a joint Ph.D. program between Xi’an Jiaotong University and Microsoft Research Asia, and was additionally supervised by Prof. Bin Shao. My research interests include generative models, geometric deep learning, and AI for Science, with current applications in quantum chemistry, especially density functional theory, and molecular structure prediction.
